Spaghetti Recipe


  • Prep: 10 mins
  • Cook: 1 hr 5 mins
  • Total: 1 hr 15 mins
  • Yield: 8 Servings
Ingredients
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 medium onion
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (16 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground pepper

Directions
  1. Combine ground beef, onion, garlic, and green pepper
    in a large saucepan over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook and stir until meat is browned and crumbly and
    vegetables are tender, 5 to 7 minutes.
  3. Drain grease.
  4. Stir diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste
    into the pan.
  5. Season with oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
  6. Simmer spaghetti sauce for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
